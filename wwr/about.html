White Water Rafting

Our purpose her at White Water Rafting is to provide everyone of all interest and skill levels the opportunity to experience the joys of white water rafting through a variety of different trips and experiences including favorites like the Black Rock River and the Calm Little Rapids.

History

Our company began as a small group of rafters out of the Colorado Mountains wanting to share our love of rafting with others. There were a few companies, but none of them seemed to fulfil our needs, so we made our own. We started with a small team of just 4 people, and we now number just over 200. Don't worry about whether or not we're experienced, because we've been rafting and exploring since '97 and we don't expect to stop anytime soon!
